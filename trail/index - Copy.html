Welcome to St. Theresa Church

Established on 7 August 1943, Senjai Parish has grown from 11 faithful members into a vibrant community of over 1,642.

Guided by devoted priests, the parish developed strong spiritual and educational foundations including the parish church, parish house, sub-station churches, and the Little Flower R.C. School.

Renovations in 1995–96 strengthened the parish, and the shrine of St. John de Britto now serves as a center for Mass and adoration.

The parish continues to flourish under the blessings of St. Thérèse of the Child Jesus — “The Little Flower.”
